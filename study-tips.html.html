10 Study Tips That Actually Work

Studying smarter, not harder, is the key to student success. Here are 10 tips that genuinely work:

  • Plan your study time and take regular breaks.
  • Use active recall instead of passive review.
  • Teach the material to someone else.
  • Try spaced repetition techniques.
  • Study in short, focused sessions (Pomodoro).
  • Eliminate distractions—use website blockers if needed.
  • Practice past papers or quiz yourself.
  • Stay hydrated and get proper sleep.
  • Switch subjects to keep your brain engaged.
  • Study in a clean, organized environment.
